The game is a turn-based combat game with different classes and actions.
Players are put onto teams of two.
Players can choose whether to attack, defend
The game ends once one team has defeated the other.
Players can also choose what class they want to play as.
Press Enter to read about class stats
Human has base stats, health: 16, strength: 16 and defence: 18.
Orc has base stats, health: 20, strength: 18 and defence: 14.
Elf has base stats, health: 15, strength: 14 and defence: 20.
Dwarf has base stats, health: 18, strength: 20 and defence: 16.
